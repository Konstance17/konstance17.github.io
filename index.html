Konstance Chen

R&D Engineer

Hello, I am Konstance. I have been working as an R&D and Mechanical Engineer for 11 years. My work encompasses the research and development of new technologies, new products, and new materials, as well as the design of mechanisms and surface decoration processes. I have extensive experience in CAD design and am familiar with various materials and manufacturing processes. Throughout my career, I have successfully led several projects from concept to market, significantly improving product performance and efficiency. Additionally, I have been furthering my education in programming languages, both front-end and back-end, and I am continuously striving to improve my skills in this area. I am passionate about innovation and always eager to embrace new challenges.
